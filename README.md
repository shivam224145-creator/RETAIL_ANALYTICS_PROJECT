# RETAIL_ANALYTICS_PROJECT
This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover insights on profitability, customer behavior, and operations. It highlights top products, loss-making items, regional performance, discount impact, and sales trends. Tools used: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Jupyter Notebook..

# 📊 Financial & Sales Analytics Project

## 🚀 Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to extract meaningful insights related to **sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and operational efficiency**. The goal is to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🧠 Key Objectives

* Identify high and low profit margin products
* Analyze regional and state-wise performance
* Understand the impact of discounts on profit
* Detect loss-making products despite high sales
* Evaluate shipping performance and delivery time
* Analyze customer segments and buying behavior
* Discover sales trends over time
* Perform market basket analysis for product combinations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python 🐍
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook

## 📊 Key Insights

### 💰 Profitability Analysis

* Some sub-categories like **Tables and Binders** generate losses
* High discounts negatively impact profit

### 🌍 Regional Insights

* States like **California and New York** drive high profits
* Some regions generate high sales but negative profit

### 🚚 Shipping Insights

* Standard Class is most used and most profitable
* Same Day shipping shows mixed profitability

### 👥 Customer Insights

* Home Office segment has highest average order value
* Top customers contribute significantly to total profit

### 📈 Time Trends

* Seasonal spikes observed in sales
* Certain months consistently perform better

### 🛒 Market Basket Analysis

* Frequently bought product combinations identified
* Useful for cross-selling and bundling strategies


## 🎯 Business Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategy to protect margins
* Focus on high-value product categories
* Improve performance of loss-making products
* Target high-value customers with personalized offers
* Use bundle strategies for frequently bought items


## 📌 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to uncover hidden patterns, improve decision-making, and enhance overall business performance.
